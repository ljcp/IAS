Our Story: Your Success

Since 1998 Industrial Air Solutions Inc. has provided sales representation for industry leading manufacturers in Michigan, Ohio and Indiana. We have provided equipment solutions for projects in many parts of the USA. Industrial Air Solutions specializes in custom engineered equipment solutions to meet the ever changing needs of our clients. We have extensive experience in air pollution control systems, heavy duty ID/FD fans for process and power generation, heat transfer and custom specialty equipment.

All the equipment we provide is built after award to stringent specifications and standards. Our clients include A/E firms, wood products manufacturers, OEM’s, steel manufacturers, 2nd /3rd tier automotive OEM’s, process ovens, parts washers and specialty production companies.
